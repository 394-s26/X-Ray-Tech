# Voice — examples (do / don't)

Each pair is one surface. The pattern is always: the wrong version sounds like a generic SaaS; the right version sounds like it was written by someone who knows the technologist's actual world.

## Homepage hero — headline

**Don't:** *Revolutionize the way you manage your healthcare credentials.*
**Do:** *Stay ahead of your IEMA renewal.*

## Homepage hero — subcopy

**Don't:** *Our AI-powered platform empowers radiologic professionals to seamlessly track their continuing education and unlock compliance success.*
**Do:** *See exactly how many of your 24 CE points you've earned this cycle, and how many days until your next renewal — in one screen.*

## Section eyebrow

**Don't:** *INTRODUCING OUR NEW DASHBOARD*
**Do:** *FOR MANAGERS*

## Feature card

**Don't:** *Powerful Insights — Leverage our analytics to gain deep visibility into your team's compliance posture.*
**Do:** *Team renewal calendar — One view of every tech's license, sorted by who's closest to expiry.*

## Primary CTA

**Don't:** *Get Started Today!*
**Do:** *Log your first certification* — or — *Try X-Ray Tech free*

## Pricing headline

**Don't:** *Flexible plans for every team*
**Do:** *Free for every X-ray tech and manager. No tiers, no trials, no card required.*

## Case study opener

**Don't:** *Discover how a leading healthcare institution transformed their credentialing workflow with X-Ray Tech.*
**Do:** *Michelle's team used to spend the first Monday of every month on a spreadsheet. They got that Monday back.*

## Email subject line

**Don't:** *Important Update: Your Credential Status*
**Do:** *Heads up — IEMA renewal in 90 days*

## Email opening line

**Don't:** *We hope this email finds you well!*
**Do:** *Quick one — your IEMA license renews on March 14, 2026. You're at 18 of 24 CE points.*

## LinkedIn post opener

**Don't:** *🚀 We're thrilled to announce…*
**Do:** *Most X-ray techs don't find out they're behind on CE credits until 30 days before their license expires. That's the problem we're fixing.*

## Instagram caption opener

**Don't:** *✨ Game-changer alert! ✨*
**Do:** *18 of 24 CE points logged. 287 days until renewal. One screen.*

## Error message

**Don't:** *Oops! Something went wrong. Please try again later.*
**Do:** *We couldn't read that PDF — try a clearer scan, or type the certification details by hand.*

## 404 page

**Don't:** *Oops! Looks like you've taken a wrong turn. 🤔*
**Do:** *This page isn't here. [Back to your dashboard]*

## About page opening

**Don't:** *X-Ray Tech is on a mission to revolutionize healthcare compliance through innovative AI-powered solutions.*
**Do:** *We built X-Ray Tech because Michelle, an X-ray imaging manager at a Chicago-area hospital, asked us to. She was tracking her team's CE credits in a spreadsheet — and so was every other manager she knew.*

## Hiring post

**Don't:** *Join our world-class team and make an impact in the healthcare space!*
**Do:** *We're looking for a designer who has worked on dashboards before — preferably for an audience of one specific type of professional. You'll work directly with Michelle and three other imaging managers on the next phase of the product.*

---

## Anti-pattern checklist

Before any X-Ray Tech copy ships — homepage, email, ad, deck slide — run it past this list. If any answer is "yes," rewrite.

- Does it contain the word *revolutionary, game-changing, supercharge, leverage, unleash, seamless, robust, AI-powered, transform, holistic, or end-to-end*?
- Does it use Title Case in a headline?
- Does it use ALL CAPS for anything more than 3 words?
- Does it have an emoji?
- Does it have an exclamation mark?
- Does it make a claim without a number or a name?
- Does it talk about "users," "customers," or "professionals" instead of "technologists" / "you"?
- Does it use the word "platform," "solution," or "ecosystem"?
- Could this exact copy ship for any healthcare-SaaS competitor without changing the brand name?

If yes to any of the above: rewrite, find the specific number or noun, and try again.
