## "Why X-Ray Tech" — three variants

### Pattern 1: three concrete capabilities

- **OCR a certificate in 30 seconds.** Upload the PDF, we read the credit type, the issuer, and the date. You confirm. Done.
- **One number for your license status.** Not "compliant" / "not compliant." A live count: *18 of 24 CE points earned.*
- **The team view managers actually wanted.** Sort by days-to-renewal. See expiring credentials 90 days out. No more spreadsheet, no more sticky notes.

### Pattern 2: three before/afters

| Before X-Ray Tech                                    | With X-Ray Tech                                     |
| ---------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Four state-board websites and a Google Sheet         | One screen                                          |
| "I think I'm fine for renewal"                       | *287 days until renewal. 6 of 24 CE points to go.*  |
| Finding out a tech's license lapsed last week        | Seeing it 90 days out, with a reminder you set      |

### Pattern 3: three pains, named

- **The license-lapse panic.** You realize on a Sunday night that you don't actually know if you have enough CE credits. We end that.
- **The Monday-morning audit.** Your director asks who's renewing this quarter. You need an answer in 30 seconds, not 30 minutes.
- **The Category-A / Category-B mix-up.** Modality-specific CE requirements are nuanced. We track which credits count toward which credentials, so you don't earn 24 of the wrong kind.

## How it works — three steps

1. **Add your license(s).** ARRT, IEMA, or whichever state board applies. We know the renewal cycles and the credit requirements.
2. **Log certifications as you complete them.** Upload the PDF, we OCR the details. You confirm. Takes 30 seconds.
3. **Watch the number go up.** Your dashboard shows your live CE point total, days until renewal, and whether each credit counts toward Category A, Category B, or your modality requirement.
