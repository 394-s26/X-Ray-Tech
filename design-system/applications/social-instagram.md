# Instagram

## Aspect ratios

| Format         | Aspect | Pixels     | Use                                          |
| -------------- | ------ | ---------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| Square post    | 1:1    | 1080×1080  | Single image (rare for us)                   |
| **Carousel**   | 4:5    | 1080×1350  | **Default.** Multi-slide thread, 3–8 frames. |
| Story / Reel   | 9:16   | 1080×1920  | Stories, Reels cover frames                  |

We optimize for 4:5 carousels. That's the workhorse.

## Carousel structure (3–8 frames)

1. **Cover (hook)** — one number or one direct question. No body copy. Lockup bottom-right.
2. **Thread (middle frames, 1–6)** — each frame teaches one small thing. One sentence at the top, supporting visual or data below.
3. **Payoff (closer)** — one CTA. "Try X-Ray Tech free" or "Read the case study at xraytech.app/michelle."

## Design rules

- **Type scale (Instagram)**: 64px headline (Geist, 600), 32px subhead, 20px body, 14px footer credit.
- **Background**: `paper` on cover and most frames. One `brand/900` frame allowed (typically the closer).
- **Color rotation**: don't rotate. Stay on `paper` + `brand/600` accents. Consistency in feed is the brand.
- **Logo placement**: bottom-right, lockup at 120px wide, except cover and closer use the stacked lockup centered.
- **Bleed**: 64px inset from every edge.

## Post types

### Single-image spotlight

A real photograph of a tech / imaging suite, with one line of overlay copy bottom-left in `display-lg` Geist, white, with a 60% ink/900 scrim under it.

### Quote card

`paper` background. Overline (brand/600) "FROM A USER". Quote in `h1` Geist, ink/900. Attribution in `body-sm` ink/500. Lockup bottom-right.

### Number card

`paper` background. `mono-xl` rendered huge (200–280px) in `brand/600`, centered. One sentence below in `h3`. Source in `caption`.

## Stories / Reels rules

- **Cover frame** is built like a 9:16 carousel cover — one number, one question, lockup bottom.
- **First frame of a Reel** is held for 1.5 seconds with the headline before any motion starts. The viewer must be able to read the hook.
- **Caption style** in Stories: avoid the "tap here ➡️" stickers. Use a single button-shape sticker if needed, in `brand/600`.
- **Music** in Reels: silent or instrumental. No trending audio that competes with the message.

## What we never post

- Memes about being a healthcare worker.
- "Diverse smiling team" stock photography.
- Anything with an emoji in the visual or caption.
- "Tag a tech who needs to see this 👇" engagement bait.
- Carousels longer than 8 frames.
- Animated text effects that draw the eye to motion instead of the message.

## Caption template

```
[One sentence that restates the cover headline in plainer language.]

[Two-to-three short sentences expanding on the why. Specific numbers, specific names.]

[One sentence with a clear next step. URL or short link.]

— X-Ray Tech
```

No hashtag chains. If you must use hashtags, pick 3 at most, all directly relevant (#ARRT #IEMA #RadiologicTechnology).
